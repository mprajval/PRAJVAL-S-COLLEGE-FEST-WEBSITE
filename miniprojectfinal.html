About KRUPAKATAKASHA COLLEGE

KRUPAKATAKASHA COLLEGE blends modern infrastructure with a strong focus on innovation, culture and sports. The campus is designed so students can learn in classrooms and also experiment on stage, in labs and on the field.

  • Smart classrooms with projectors and audio systems.
  • Computer labs equipped for coding, design and research.
  • Amphitheatre and auditorium for cultural events and fests.
  • Dedicated grounds for football, basketball and athletics.
  • Clubs for music, dance, drama, coding, photography and more.

Neon Fest is used as a practical learning platform where students apply event management, design, marketing and technical skills in a real environment.
